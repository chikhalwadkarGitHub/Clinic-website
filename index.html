About the Clinic

At Dr. Arya's Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Clinic, we believe movement is medicine. Our state-of-the-art facility in Ravet offers comprehensive care in Orthopaedic Rehabilitation, Sports Injury Management, Women’s Health Physiotherapy, Neurological Rehabilitation, Paediatric Care, and Post-Operative Recovery.

We combine modern techniques, advanced equipment and affordable, one-on-one sessions to reduce pain, restore function, and improve quality of life. Whether you are recovering from an injury or simply aiming for an active lifestyle—feel strong, live strong.
